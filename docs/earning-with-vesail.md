# Earning with veSAIL

## What is veSAIL?

Full Sail is a decentralized exchange (DEX) on Sui. Its governance token, SAIL, can be locked for up to 4 years to obtain veSAIL (voting escrow SAIL). The longer you lock, the more voting power you receive:

- 1 SAIL locked for 4 years = 1 veSAIL
- 1 SAIL locked for 2 years = 0.5 veSAIL

Voting power decays over time as the lock approaches expiration. **Auto-Max lock** is enabled by default, keeping your lock at 4 years so voting power does not decay. You can toggle it off if you want your lock to expire.

## How You Earn

veSAIL holders earn revenue from two sources:

### 1. Passive revenue (no action required)

A portion of all trading fees collected by the protocol is distributed to every veSAIL holder, proportional to their voting power. You do not need to vote or take any action — simply holding veSAIL entitles you to this yield.

Currently, 80% of all trading fees go to passive revenue and 20% go to active voters. Passive revenue accumulates every epoch (one week) and can be claimed at any time.

### 2. Active Voting Rewards (requires voting)

Each week, veSAIL holders can predict trading volume for pools in the upcoming epoch. Voters who make accurate predictions earn a larger share of the active portion of trading fees.

**How it works:**
1. During the voting period (Thursday to Wednesday), submit volume predictions for one or more pools.
2. Your rewards depend on prediction accuracy — closer predictions earn a higher reward multiplier.
3. You vote during one week, and trading fee rewards become claimable at the end of the following week.

**Key point:** If you do not vote, you receive zero active rewards for that epoch. Passive revenue still applies.

## Summary

| Revenue Source | Requires Voting? | What You Earn | Timing |
|---|---|---|---|
| Passive revenue | No | 80% of trading fees | Claimable each epoch |
| Active Voting | Yes | 20% of Trading fees | ~1 week after voting |

## Getting Started

1. **Acquire SAIL** — buy or earn it.
2. **Lock SAIL into veSAIL** — choose a lock duration (longer = more voting power). Auto-Max lock is enabled by default, keeping your lock at 4 years with non-decaying power.
3. **Earn passively** — passive revenue accrues automatically.
4. **Vote for extra yield** — submit volume predictions each week to earn active voting rewards on top of passive income.
